BookSwap






This web application is for University Missouri Students that are tired of paying too much money for textbooks every semester.
Create an account by putting in your email and a password and then clicking "Create Account". Once you have an account
you can search for a book that you need or submit a book you are trying to get rid of. The only public contact information
that can be found through the site are emails so you dont need to worry about not being able to contact the person with the book.
